Professional Mediation &
ADR Services

PMA provides a comprehensive range of mediation, training, and advisory services to help individuals, organizations, and institutions resolve disputes effectively and build a culture of dialogue.

Our Core Services

Commercial & Corporate Mediation

Resolving commercial disputes, contract conflicts, shareholder issues, and business disagreements.

Court-Referred Mediation

Mediation services aligned with judicial processes to support faster and amicable resolutions.

Family & Community Mediation

Helping families and communities resolve disputes with empathy, privacy, and understanding.

Workplace & Organizational Disputes

Addressing workplace conflicts, employment issues, and internal organizational matters.

IMI-Certified Mediator Training

Professional training programs certified by the International Mediation Institute (IMI).

Workshops & Awareness Sessions

Customized workshops and awareness sessions for institutions, professionals, and communities.

Institutional & Policy Advisory

Advisory services for institutions and policymakers to strengthen ADR systems and frameworks.
