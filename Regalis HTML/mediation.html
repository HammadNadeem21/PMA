What is Mediation?

Mediation is a voluntary, confidential, and structured process where a neutral mediator helps the parties in conflict to communicate, understand each other's perspectives and reach a mutually acceptable solution.

It empowers the parties to shape the outcome, preserves relationships and saves time, cost and emotional stress.

Why Choose Mediation with PMA?

Faster & Cost-Effective

Resolve disputes in weeks, not years.

Confidential

Private process with no public records.

Relationship-Preserving

Focus on mutual understanding and win-win solutions.

Flexible & Collaborative

Parties control the outcome.

IMI-Certified Standards

Internationally recognized mediator accreditation.

PMA is Pakistan's first and oldest dedicated organization for mediation accreditation, professional training, and advocacy — established in 2013–2014 and based in Karachi.

How Mediation Works?

1

Submit Your Request

You contact PMA to share the details of your dispute.

2

Initial Case Assessment

We review the matter and determine its suitability for mediation.

3

Mediator Assignment

A neutral and qualified mediator is assigned to your case.

4

Facilitated Sessions

The mediator facilitates structured dialogue to explore solutions.

5

Resolution & Agreement

Parties reach a mutually acceptable outcome and formalize the agreement.
